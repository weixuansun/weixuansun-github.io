Weixuan Sun (孙伟轩)

I am a Ph.D. student in Australian National University in Canberra, Australia, supervised by Prof. Nick Barnes. I am currently doing research intern at Shanghai AI Lab in Shanghai. I have also worked in SenseTime and OPPO as research intern. I received my Bachelors from Beijing Institute of Technology(mechanics) and Australian National University(mechatronics). My research interests include, effieicnt transformer networks, large language model, weakly supervised semantic segmentation and multimodal learning.
